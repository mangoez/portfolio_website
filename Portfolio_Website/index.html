Who I am

Brain Computer Interface enthusiast

Po is an electrical systems graduate pursuing an honours in neural engineering with an interest in rehabilitation; specifically Brain Computer Interfaces. In combination with robotics he aspires to integrate machine learning and neuroscience to improve the quality of life for people who suffer from cognitive and neuromuscular disorders.

Outside of my role in the Student Science Squad, I mentor for In2science, hoping to show students that studying STEM is an opportunity to aspire to, not an obstacle to avoid.
